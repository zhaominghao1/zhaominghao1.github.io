About Me

Hi there, I am a fourth year undergraduate student double majoring in Applied Mathematics and Economics. I have a strong foundation in Calculus, Econometrics/Statistics, Probability, and Numerical Optimization. I am also familiar with Python, R, Java, STATA, MATLAB, and Latex. Throughout my undergraduate career, I have gained research experience from working as a Research Assistant in the Economics department under the supervision of PhD Candidate Philip Hoxie My work mostly ranges from data collection, analysis to literature review. I also have conducted a number of independent projects focusing on Causal Inference, Economic Forecasting, Policy Analysis and Game Theory. I am passionate about using my skills in Mathematics and Computation to solve problems in Health Economics. I have strong interests in teaching, and have served as a teaching assistant for various Econ and Math courses in UC San Diego.
